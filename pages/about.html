About Da Capo Music

Thank you for visiting Da Capo Music!

My name is Dan, and I’m a Chicago-based professional clarinetist. As with many of my clarinet colleagues, I have collected a large amount of clarinet equipment over the years, including vintage and contemporary mouthpieces, barrels, ligatures, bells, and instruments—I’ve always been looking for “the perfect setup”.

After years of playing with some of the top ensembles all over the country and testing out various combinations of equipment, I have finally found my ideal setup: Vandoren BD5 regular mouthpiece, Vandoren Blue Box No. 4, and Rover Eddie Daniels II ligature with metal plate . It’s been working very well for me for a while, and I've been happy with it.

So, what would I do with all of the equipment and instruments, which I’ve carefully been guarding for decades? This website is the solution—why not make them available on a single platform for purchase at an affordable price, instead of listing individually on eBay or Facebook? Needless to say, it’s been fun building this website from scratch; I hope you like what you see here.

A few other things I’d like to mention:

  • Every item on this site was hand-picked by me through a meticulous testing process .
  • I will be updating the site regularly and sending email reminders, so please sign up to be on my mailing list using the form at the bottom of the site.
  • If you have any questions, please use this form, and I’d be more than happy to assist!

Thanks!
Daniel Won
